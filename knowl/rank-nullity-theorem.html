Theorem 4.5.3. (Rank-Nullity Theorem).

Let \(V\) and \(W\) be vector spaces over a field \(F\text{.}\) Let \(T\colon V\to W\) be an \(F\)-linear map. If \(V\) is a finite-dimensional vector space over \(F\) then,
\begin{equation*} \dim_FV=\dim_F\ker(T)+\dim_F\Im(T)={\rm nullity}(T)+{\rm rank}(T). \end{equation*}
