1. Show that any \(F\)-linear transformation maps the zero vector to the zero vector.
  2. Let \(T\colon V\to W\) be an \(F\)-linear transformation. Show that for every \(v\in V\) and any \(\alpha_i\in F\)
    \begin{equation*} T(\sum\alpha_iv_i)=\sum\alpha_iT(v_i)\text{.} \end{equation*}
  3. Show that \(T(v_1-v_2)=T(v_1)-T(v_2)\text{.}\)
  4. Show that a composition of \(F\)-linear transformation is an \(F\)-linear transformation.
