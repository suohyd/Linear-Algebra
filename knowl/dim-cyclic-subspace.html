Proposition 7.5.5.

Let \(V\) be a finite-dimensional vector space over a field \(F\) and let \(T\colon V\to V\) be an \(F\)-linear map. The cyclic subspace \(W\) generated by the principal vector \(e\) of order \(m\) with an eigenvalue \(\lambda\) is invariant under \(T\) and \(\dim_FW=m\text{.}\)
