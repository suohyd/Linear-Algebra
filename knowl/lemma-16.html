Lemma 7.5.9.

Let \(J_\lambda\in M_n(F)\) be a Jordan block.
  1. The characteristic polynomial of \(J_\lambda\text{,}\) \(\chi(t)=(t-\lambda)^n\text{.}\)
  2. The trace of \(J_\lambda\text{,}\) \(tr(J_\lambda)=n\lambda\) and the determinant of \(J_\lambda\text{,}\) \(\det(J_\lambda)=\lambda^n\text{.}\)
  3. We have \(\chi(t)=t^n-tr(J_\lambda)+\cdots+(-1)^n\det(J_\lambda)\text{.}\)
  4. The minimal polynomial of \(J_{\lambda}\) coincides with the characteristic polynomial of \(J_{\lambda}\text{.}\)
