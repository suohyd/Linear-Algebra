Definition 7.2.17. (Minimal polynomial).

Let \(V\) be a finite-dimensional vector space over a field \(F\text{,}\) and let \(T\colon V\to V\) be an \(F\)-linear map. The minimal polynomial of \(T\) is the monic polynomial \(m_T(t)\in F[t]\) of least degree annihilating \(T\text{.}\)
Let \(A\in M_n(F)\text{.}\) The minimal polynomial of \(A\) is the monic polynomial \(m_A(t)\in F[t]\) of least degree annihilating \(A\text{.}\)
