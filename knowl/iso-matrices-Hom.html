Theorem 5.2.3.

Let \(V\) and \(W\) be vector spaces over a field \(F\text{.}\) Fix bases \(\mathfrak{B}_V\) and \(\mathfrak{B}_W\) of \(V\) and \(W\text{,}\) respectively. Further assume that \(\dim_FV=n\) and \(\dim_FW=m\text{.}\) Consider the map
\begin{equation} \ell\colon M_{m\times n}(F)\to\Hom_F(V,W)\quad\text{given by}\quad A\mapsto\ell_A\tag{5.2.3} \end{equation}
Here \(\ell_A\) is as defined in (5.2.2). The map \(\ell\) is an \(F\)-isomorphism.
