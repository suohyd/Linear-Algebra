Exercise 5.

Let \(U,V,\) and \(W\) be finite-dimensional vector spaces over a field \(F\) with bases \(\mathfrak{B}_U,\mathfrak{B}_V\text{,}\) and \(\mathfrak{B}_W\) respectively. Consider \(F\)-linear maps \(T\colon U\to V\) and \(S\colon V\to W\text{.}\) Show that the matrix of \(S\circ T\) with respect to bases \(\mathfrak{B}_U\) and \(\mathfrak{B}_W\) is \([S]_{\mathfrak{B}_V}^{\mathfrak{B}_W}\cdot[T]_{\mathfrak{B}_U}^{\mathfrak{B}_V}\text{.}\)
