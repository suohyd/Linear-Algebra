Lemma 5.4.3.

Let \(A\in M_{m\times n}(F)\) and \(E\) be an \(n\times n\) elementary matrix over \(F\text{.}\) Then, \(rrk(A)=rrk(EA)\) and \(crk(A)=crk(AE)\text{.}\) Moreover, elementary column operations (resp., row operations) does not change the row rank (resp., column rank) of \(A\text{.}\)
