Definition 4.1.6.

The set of all \(F\)-linear mappings between vector spaces \(V\) and \(W\) over \(F\) is denoted by \(\Hom_F(V,W)\) or when the context is clear by \(\Hom(V,W)\text{.}\) We denote \(\Hom_F(V,V)\) by \(\End_F(V)\text{.}\) The set \(\Hom_F(V,F)\) is called the dual space of \(V\text{,}\) and it is denoted by \(V^*\text{.}\) The dual space \(V^*\) is in fact a vector space over \(F\) (see RemarkÂ 4.1.7). An element of \(V^*\) is called a linear functional.
