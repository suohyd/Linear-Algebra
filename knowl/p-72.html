Let \(n\geq 2\) be a natural number. Consider \(n\times n\) matrices over a field \(F\text{,}\) \(M_n(F)\text{.}\) Let \(E_{ij}\in M_n(F)\) be the matrix with \((i,j)\)-th entry \(1\) and all other entries zero. We have observed that \(\{E_{ij}:1\leq i,j\leq n\}\) is a basis for \(M_n(F)\text{.}\) Recall that we defined the trace of map in ExampleÂ 4.2.5 as follows.
\begin{equation*} tr\colon M_n(F)\to F\quad\text{given by}\quad(a_{ij})\mapsto\sum_i a_{ii}. \end{equation*}
