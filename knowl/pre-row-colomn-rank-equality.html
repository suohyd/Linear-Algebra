Theorem 5.3.7.

Let \(V, W\) be finite-dimensional vector spaces over a field \(F\) and let \(T\colon V\to W\) be an \(F\)-linear map. Then there exists bases in which the matrix of \(T\) has a block-diagonal form
\begin{equation*} \begin{pmatrix}I_r\amp 0_1\\0_2\amp 0_3\end{pmatrix}, \end{equation*}
where \(r=\dim_F\Im(T)\) and \(I_r\) is \(r\times r\) identity matrix, and \(0_i\) are zero matrices of appropriate size.
