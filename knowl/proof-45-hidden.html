Let \(\dim_FV=n\text{.}\) By CorollaryÂ 5.2.4, \(\dim_F\End_F(V)=(\dim_FV)^2<\infty\text{.}\) Hence the subset \(\{\unit_V,T,T^2,\ldots,T^n,\ldots,T^{n^2}\}\) of \(\End_F(V)\) is linearly dependent, i.e., there are scalars \(a_0,a_1,\ldots,a_{n^2}\in F\text{,}\) not all zero, such that
\begin{equation*} a_0\unit_V+a_1T+\cdots + a_{n^2}T^{n^2}=0\in\End_FV. \end{equation*}
Hence the polynomial \(p(t)=a_0+a_t+\cdots+a_{n^2}t^{n^2}\in F[t]\) is an annihilating polynomial of \(T\text{.}\)
