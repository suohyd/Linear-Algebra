Definition 7.2.2. (Eigenvector and Eigenvalue of a square matrix).

Let \(A\in M_n(F)\) be an \(n\times n\) matrix over a field \(F\text{.}\) A nonzero column vector is an eigenvector of \(A\) if it is an eigenvector of a linear map \(\ell_A\colon F^n\to F^n\) defined by the left multiplication by \(A\text{.}\) The scalar corresponding to an eigenvector of \(\ell_A\) is called an eigenvalue of \(A\text{.}\)
