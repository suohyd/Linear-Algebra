Remark 5.2.1.

Suppose that \(\mathfrak{B}_V=(v_1,\ldots,v_n)\) and \(\mathfrak{B}_W=(w_1,\ldots,w_m)\) are ordered bases for \(V\) and \(W\text{,}\) respectively. The composition \({}_nT\circ T_{\mathfrak{B}_V}\colon V\to M_{n\times 1}(F)\) in the eq. (5.2.2) is given by
\begin{equation*} \sum\alpha_iv_i\xrightarrow{{}_nT\circ T_{\mathfrak{B}_V}}\begin{pmatrix}\alpha_1\\\alpha_2\\\vdots\\\alpha_n\end{pmatrix}. \end{equation*}
While the composition \(T^{-1}_{\mathfrak{B}_W}\circ {}_mT^{-1}\colon M_{m\times 1}(F)\to W\) in eq. (5.2.2) is given by
\begin{equation*} \begin{pmatrix}\beta_1\\\beta_2\\\vdots\\\beta_m\end{pmatrix}\xrightarrow{T^{-1}_{\mathfrak{B}_V}\circ {}_mT^{-1}}\sum\beta_iw_i. \end{equation*}
