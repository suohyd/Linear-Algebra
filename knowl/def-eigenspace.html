Definition 7.2.11. (Eigenspace).

Let \(V\) be a finite-dimensional vector space over a field \(F\) and let \(T\colon V\to V\) be an \(F\)-linear map. Suppose that \(\lambda\in F\) is an eigenvalue of \(T\text{.}\) The subspace \(\ker(T-\lambda\unit_V)\) is called the eigenspace corresponding to eigenvalue \(\lambda\).
